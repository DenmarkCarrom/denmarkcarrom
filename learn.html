What is Carrom?

Carrom is one of the most popular indoor games in India. Once treated a pastime game limited to indoor home plays, it is now played at competitive level in an organized manner. It is a scientific game and is based on physics and geometry. It requires thorough knowledge of angles, deftness of touch, keen sight and superlative control of nerves besides mental and physical fitness. It also requires skill and deep concentration.
